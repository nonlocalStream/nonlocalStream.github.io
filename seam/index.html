Horizontal Carving

Explanation of the algorithm:

I use the e1 energy function. The e1 energy matrix is gained by adding up the gradient energy gx and gradient energy gy. gx = the absolute value of the grayscaled image passed by a [-1,0,1] filter. gx = the absolute value of the grayscaled image passed by a [-1;0;1] filter.

After mapping the original image to its gradient energy matrix. We get a vertical seam by using dynamic programming to calulcate the seam that minimize the sum of energy. This would be the most unnoticeable seam that can be removed from the picture to reduce the with.
